New Housing Price Index by Province

Source: Statistics Canada, Table 18-10-0205-01 · December 2016 = 100

The New Housing Price Index (NHPI) tracks changes in the selling prices of new residential houses over time, indexed to December 2016 = 100. Higher values indicate faster price growth. Quebec and Manitoba have seen the steepest increases since 2020, while Alberta and Saskatchewan remain closest to their 2016 baseline.
