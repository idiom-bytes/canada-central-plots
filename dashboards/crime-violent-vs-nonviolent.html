Source: Statistics Canada, Table 35-10-0026-01

The Crime Severity Index broken down into violent and non-violent components. Violent CSI captures severity-weighted crimes against persons, while non-violent captures property crime, drug offences, and other non-person crimes. Both are indexed to 100 for Canada in 2006.
