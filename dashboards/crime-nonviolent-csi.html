Non-Violent Crime Severity Index by Province

Source: Statistics Canada, Table 35-10-0026-01

The Non-Violent Crime Severity Index measures police-reported property and other non-violent crime. Saskatchewan leads nationally, while Quebec has seen the steepest decline — now below 50, half the 2006 baseline.
