Violent Crime Severity Index

Source: Statistics Canada, Table 35-10-0026-01

The Violent Crime Severity Index measures police-reported violent crime weighted by offence seriousness. Manitoba and Saskatchewan consistently report rates 2x the national average, while PEI and Quebec are the safest.
