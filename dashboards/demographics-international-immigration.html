International Immigration

Source: Statistics Canada, Table 17-10-0014-01

Annual international immigrants arriving in each province. The per-capita view (per 1,000 population) reveals that smaller provinces like Manitoba, New Brunswick, and PEI receive proportionally high immigration relative to their size. Ontario receives ~40% of all immigrants in raw numbers but is closer to the national average per capita.
