Median Weekly Wages by Province

Source: Statistics Canada, Table 14-10-0064-01

Median weekly wages for employees across all industries, showing how worker compensation has evolved over 29 years. Alberta has historically led in wages due to the oil and gas sector, but BC and Ontario have closed the gap. Atlantic provinces have seen the fastest percentage growth since 2020.
