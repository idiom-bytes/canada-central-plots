Interprovincial Migration by Province

Source: Statistics Canada, Table 17-10-0045-01

Net interprovincial migration — the number of Canadians moving to a province minus those leaving. Positive values mean a province is gaining residents from other provinces; negative means it's losing them. Alberta has been the #1 destination since 2020, while Ontario has been losing residents to other provinces despite strong international immigration.
