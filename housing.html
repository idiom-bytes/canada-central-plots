Housing Starts & New Housing Price Index

Source: StatCan Table 34-10-0135 (CMHC) & Table 18-10-0205 (NHPI)

Housing starts measure the number of new residential construction projects begun each year — the primary indicator of housing supply response to demand. The New Housing Price Index (NHPI) tracks price changes for new homes (Dec 2016 = 100). Together, these metrics reveal whether provinces are building enough to keep pace with population growth and demand, or falling behind.
