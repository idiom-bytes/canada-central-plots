Housing Supply & Prices by Province

Source: CMHC Table 34-10-0135 & StatCan Table 18-10-0205

Housing starts per 10,000 population measures new construction activity normalized by population size, making provinces directly comparable regardless of size. Alberta consistently builds at nearly double the national rate, while Ontario has fallen to the lowest per-capita starts in the country. The New Housing Price Index (NHPI, Dec 2016 = 100) tracks price trends for new homes.
