Rental Vacancy Rate by Province

Source: CMHC / Statistics Canada

The rental vacancy rate measures the percentage of purpose-built rental apartments that are unoccupied. A healthy vacancy rate is typically 3-5% — below 3% indicates a tight market with upward pressure on rents, while above 5% suggests softer demand. Canada's vacancy rate hit historic lows in 2023 as immigration surged beyond housing supply capacity.
