Methodology

Layer 1: Peer Rank

Where does the province rank among all 10 provinces? Same as v1 scorecard.

Weight: 40%

Layer 2: Historical Trajectory

EMA(3y) vs EMA(10y) trend + current value as percentile in own 20-year range. Are things getting better or worse for this province?

Weight: 30% (trend) + 30% (percentile)

Layer 3: National Health

Canada's own 10-year trajectory sets a grade ceiling. If the national metric is stagnant or declining, no province can score above B or C — because being #1 in a declining pool isn't truly good.

Modifier: caps maximum grade
